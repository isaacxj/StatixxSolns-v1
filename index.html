About

A small company, on purpose

Statixx Solutions is a software company run by engineers. The name comes from static, because that is how most projects arrive: a lot of noise and no clear picture. We spent years building and testing software at larger companies, and we started Statixx to do that work directly for the clients who need it.

Being small is the point. When you hire us, the person you talk to is the person writing the code. Projects move faster, questions get straight answers, and nothing gets lost between departments.

We work remotely with startups turning an idea into a working product and with small businesses that need their website, store, or internal tools built right the first time.
